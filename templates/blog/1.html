मामका: पाण्डवाश्चैव किमकुर्वत सञ्जय ||1||

Translation: Dhritarashtra said: O Sanjaya, after my sons and the sons of Pandu assembled in the place of pilgrimage at Kurukshetra, desiring to fight, what did they do?
